Why Kutoring

Not just translation. Real help from real people.

Many services translate words. Kutoring focuses on situations — when a student needs someone who can explain, guide, accompany, or solve a problem together.

When Korean systems feel complicated

Hospital reservations, school notices, immigration tasks, and public offices can be hard to handle alone. Kutoring turns those moments into simple requests.

Find help by situation

Students can request help by category instead of searching random posts or group chats.

Talk before meeting

Real-time chat and video call make it easier to explain the problem before getting help.

Designed for trust

Reviews, reports, and admin tools help build a safer environment for both students and helpers.

How it works

From request to support in four steps.

Kutoring keeps the flow simple so students can get help without learning a complicated system.

1

Choose a category

Select hospital, paperwork, translation, campus life, or local help.

2

Post a request

Write what happened, where help is needed, and when it should be done.

3

Match with a helper

Connect with a Korean helper who can understand the request.

4

Chat, call, and review

Use chat or video call, complete the help, then leave a review.

App experience

One app for the small problems that become big abroad.

Kutoring is built around practical moments: asking for help, understanding Korean, finding campus information, and communicating with helpers in real time.

01

Help request matching

Students can create requests for hospital visits, paperwork, translation, and everyday errands.

02

Real-time chat and video call

Matched users can communicate through chat and video call before or during help.

03

Community and campus information

Students can share information and check useful campus content such as notices and cafeteria menus.

04

Review, report, and admin management

Review and report features support safer matching, while admin tools help manage service operations.
